Nocturne: Terminal Reality


Some interesting facts about the game I played.

  1. The Infernal Engine: the engine Nocturne uses, has also been incorporated into another famous or infamous game, the first Bloodrayne and its second iteration.
  2. There was a planned sequel to Nocturne, involving one of the main characters from the first mission of the game: Svetlana, but that would then evolve into the Bloodrayne games.
  3. GoD publishing, the main publishing company behind publishing the game, has also been responsible for giving other titles the marketing for their success.
  4. Under their publishing, titles like Serious Sam, Max Payne, Mafia, and Oni; a game of Bungie's creation, have been published and marketed under this publisher.

The Basic Premise: Nocturne is a 3rd person action horror game where you play as an agent working for a secret, paranormal government organization known as Spookhouse. Spookhouse's responsibility is the extirpation and extermination of any supernatural threat, and the Stranger; the most skilled operative in the entire organization, has been tasked with many missions that you play throughout the game.

System Shock: Nice Jump Human

Perhaps one of the best older RPG's I've played. I'd rank it higher than the first Fallout in my earnest opinion.

A game critically acclaimed and loved by all for being one of the first "hybrids" between the FPS and RPG/dungeon crawling genres. System Shock 1 was released on September 23rd, 1994 by Looking Glass Studio; who are the same people behind the Thief series and then went on to develop other games like Bioshhock. Taking place in the near-future of 2072, a corrupt CEO by the name of Edward Diego hires a hacker who is captured by private security forces, after the hacker attempted to break through Tri-Optimum Corporation's networks. After being captured, Edward Diego promises to let the Hacker be free if he were to hack SHODAN: the AI controlling Citadel Station, whihch was in orbit around Saturn, by disabling its moral and ethical restrictions. The Hacker accepts and is given a military-grade neural implant in the medical bay of Citadel Station, where the main events of the game begin.

The final stage of System Shock 1. There are 9 levels within the game's main location of Citadel Station, with the very last one being the place where you inevitably confront SHODAN.

The first starting level/dungeon of System Shock 1. It's a great introductory level where you can familiarize and spend time with learning the mechanics of the game, and getting comfortable to the very awkward controls of the game.

Battle Angel Alita: My favorite OVA

From the creator of the Battle Angel Alita manga or Gunnm in Japanese, is perhaps one of my favorite OVA's of all time. Battle Angel Alita is a post-apocalyptic cyberpunk dystopian anime that chronicles the same series of events as the manga, save without continuing any further after volume 1 of the manga. The manga itself is a must read in my opinion and I already own and have read the first volume of it, with me planning on to buy subsequent chapters.

I also highly recommend watching the OVA and even the live action movie. Normally, live action adaptations of anime are not well received, but the live-action version of Battle Angel Alita was created by James Cameron: the director of the Terminator movies.

Killer7

Silver Case and Michigan: Report From Hell, are credited as being the first and perhaps best works created by Suda51: also known to be responsible for creating the No More Heroes games which has just recently had its third entry in the franchise.

Killer7 is an on-rails first-person-shooter set in an early, alternate version of the 21st century, where all international disputes have been solved by a stronger version of the United Nations. All nuclear weapons have been either disarmed or launched into the Earth's atmosphere to collide with one another.

Despite this superficial utopia, the world government's are far more authoritarian and have banned the Internet to prevent the spread of "harmful ideologies" from disrupting the social order. However, this has not stopped a terrorist organization known as the Heaven's Smiles from committing terrorist attacks around the world.

With members of the Heaven's Smiles having subjected themselves to being infected by a strange viral pathogen, they have become virtually invisible and the only people in the world capable of killing and stopping them would be a group known as the Killer7: professional hitmen with supernatural abilities who are the only ones with the power to stop Heaven's Smiles.
